Profile

I am a Master student of Economics at the WiSo Faculty, University of Cologne, with a focus on market design, behaviour, and applied data science.

After completing my B.Sc. in Economics at Bonn University, I pursued research at the intersection of econometric theory and machine learning. My undergraduate thesis, "Comparison of Discriminant Analysis and Random Forests in Classification", was written at the IFS.

Academic Interests

  • Labour economics & intergenerational mobility
  • Panel econometrics & model selection
  • Data-driven policy analysis
